How to measure pressure correctly?

One of the most important indicators of human health is his blood pressure. Pressure monitoring will help to detect the development of dangerous diseases in time and take timely measures for their treatment and prevention.

In order to control blood pressure, it is necessary not only to purchase a reliable and accurate device for measuring it, but also to know how to measure blood pressure correctly.

OMRON M3 Expert automatic tonometer with calculation of the average value

Rules for measuring blood pressure
It is very important to observe the rules of pressure measurement, because the accuracy of the measurement results depends on it. The existing rules are quite simple and their implementation will not cause difficulties even with self-measurement of blood pressure.

So, the measurement rules:

Before measuring the pressure, you should not eat, drink caffeinated drinks, smoke, or drink alcohol. If the above actions have been performed, then it is worth starting the pressure measurement no earlier than after 1.5 hours.
It is recommended to measure pressure sitting or lying down. It is necessary to take a relaxed pose – lean your back against the back of an armchair or chair, put your hand on a table or other surface so that the cuff is at the level of the heart.
The pressure is measured on the non-working hand (for right–handers – left, for left-handers - right). The correct location of the cuff on the arm is important. The cuff should follow the contour of the arm, tightly covering it. The OMRON fan-shaped cuff fits well, providing uniform compression, which is important for accurate and comfortable measurement. The lower edge of the cuff should be 2 cm above the elbow bend.
During the measurement, you should not move, talk, be distracted by extraneous stimuli – TV, radio, household conversations and telephone conversations, loud music and the like.
The pressure should be measured three times with an interval of 2 minutes. If the blood pressure level is normal, then measuring once a day will be enough to monitor blood pressure. If the pressure level deviates from the norm, then it is necessary to monitor its fluctuations, measuring three times a day - in the morning, afternoon and evening.
Features of measuring pressure with a wrist tonometer
OMRON R2 automatic wrist tonometer, easy to use, accurate result
Automatic blood pressure monitors on the wrist are very convenient to use. They are exactly as good as traditional shoulder tonometers, but at the same time they are light and mobile – you can easily take them with you to the gym, to work, for a walk, on a trip. The accuracy of the measurement results with a wrist tonometer strongly depends on how accurately the measurement rules were followed.

When using wrist tonometers, it is necessary to follow the general recommendations listed above. The edge of the cuff should be above the bone on the arm.

You need to put your hand with a tonometer in the middle of the shoulder of the other hand, at the level of the heart. With the other hand, you need to press the "Start" button, and then hold the hand with the tonometer by the elbow until it stops measuring.

Compliance with all measurement rules will allow you to get accurate results of the blood pressure level, be aware of its fluctuations and take timely measures that can save health and life.
