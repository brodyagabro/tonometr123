Instructions for the use of a mechanical tonometer

The instructions below will help anyone understand how to measure pressure with a mechanical tonometer: A person should sit on a chair with his back and legs straight. Next, you need to put your hand on the table, on which the pressure measurement with a tonometer will take place. If a person is right-handed, it is better to carry out the procedure on the left limb, and vice versa. For a more accurate result, doctors advise taking measurements on both hands in turn. A cuff 3 cm from the elbow bend is put on the arm and secured with Velcro. The stethoscope membrane is applied to the elbow bend area. With the help of a pear, the air supply begins to the cuff. You need to stop pumping it 30 units after the last pulse beat you heard. After reaching the required point, you need to turn the wheel a little under the pear. The air should come out no faster than 2 units per second. The first blow heard will be the upper pressure indicator, and the last one will be the lower one. Such an algorithm of actions represents an auscultative measurement technique invented by N. S. Korotkov back in the early twentieth century. In the absence of a stethoscope, the question of how to measure blood pressure becomes more relevant. The palpatory method of determining values can help here. The technique of execution as a whole will be identical to the auscultative type, but in this case, instead of a stethoscope, the index and middle finger are used. The measurement accuracy does not change and the palpatory method is well suited for people who have hearing problems. Their list looks like this: Before measuring the baby's blood pressure, he needs to be told about this procedure and asked to wait a couple of minutes. Usually this method helps, but in the case of children it is better to use automatic tonometers, as they show the result faster. Even the most modern tonometers will not show the exact numbers if a person is cold or hot. In one case, the vessels narrow and the pressure rises, and in the other they expand and it falls. Before measuring blood pressure, it is advisable to rest for half an hour to calm down and relax.
